Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

Aircraft Selection

How does the jet selection process work?

Our advanced algorithm considers your specific requirements including budget, passenger count, and desired flight range to recommend the perfect aircraft for your needs.

What types of aircraft are available?

We offer a wide range of private jets from light jets to ultra-long-range aircraft, accommodating 4-19 passengers with varying range capabilities.

Purchase Process

What are the financing options?

We offer various financing solutions including outright purchase, lease options, and fractional ownership programs to suit different financial preferences.

How long does the purchase process take?

The typical purchase process takes 4-8 weeks, including aircraft inspection, documentation, and delivery arrangements.

Technical Support

What maintenance support is provided?

We provide comprehensive maintenance support including scheduled maintenance, 24/7 technical assistance, and global support network access.

How do I schedule maintenance?

Maintenance can be scheduled through our online portal or by contacting our support team directly.
